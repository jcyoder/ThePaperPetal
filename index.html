Welcome

The Paper Petal is a small company located in Greeley, Colorado, that specializes in paper art. We pride ourselves in handmade invitations, scrapbooks, greeting cards, and ornaments. One of our specialties is the creation of quilled artwork on photo mats. All quilled work at The Paper Petal is done by hand with much love and patience.
