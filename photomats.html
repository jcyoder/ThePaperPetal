Quilled Photo Mats

Your precious memories should be displayed for your daily enjoyment. Many families spend hundreds or thousands of dollars on professional photos. Don't hide them in a shoe box!

We will customize the mat colors and quilled artwork to match your photo. Are you interested in adding names and dates onto the mat? We can provide you with some options and pricing to make that happen.


Need help finding a frame?

Due to the 3D artwork, a shadowbox frame under two inches in depth will be required. Let us know if you would like help with your frame purchase.

Pricing will vary depending on material cost and the complexity of the design. A deposit for the material cost is required to initiate the project.
