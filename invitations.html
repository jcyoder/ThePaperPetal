Designing Your Custom Invitation

Based on your theme, colors, invitation size, style and any other requirements, three designs will be created for your review. You can select the design you like best and we can tweak the design from there. If you don't like what you see, you can walk away and only owe money for the design work. If you like the design, then we can move forward with price estimates to make that invitation.

Design work is done by an hourly rate. Once we move forward with making the invitations, a fixed price will be used for each invitation, response card, etc...
